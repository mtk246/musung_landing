How a MUSUNG Group Will Help You Reach Your Goals

CORPORATE RESPONSIBILITY

We has decades of expertise in readymade garment manufacturing which began with the philosophy of expertise, excellence, and elegance. As a leading forerunner of garment industry, we offer unparalleled services and receive satisfaction from clients by providing exceptional quality of products that are attentively manufactured, inspected, and distributed by our seasoned employees who have paramount specialty in garment industry. With our state-of-the-art and high-productivity manufacturing system in our three independent factory facilities; Musung, New Green Land, and Mega One, we rely on built in mechanism to process order, manufacture products, and distribute overseas.

Having employee educational training facility within our factory, we pride ourselves in forging impeccable workforce whom are responsible for serving the best due diligence for our clients. We take clients’ expectations as our top priorities and we provide foremost work environments for employees by carefully looking over their concerns. As Musung in Korean means “limitless prolificacy”, we are striving to achieve unprecedented accomplishments and to build everlasting community that lasts over centuries.

INITIATIVE

We invest in our communities with our non-profit organization to provide essential solutions that respond to regional growth.

We constantly seek new opportunities to help regional economy through employment and development.

STRENGTHENING COMMUNITIES

We believe we have a fundamental responsibility to help our clients and communities.

We actively take initiative to reach fair distribution of wealth by providing favorable work conditions to economically marginalized group; pregnant women, workers with disability, and illiterate employees.

We further educate and train our employees to be the next leaders of industry as our business philosophy is deeply rooted in altruism.

ENVIRONMENTAL SUSTAINABILITY

Our leading scale and expertise help us reduce environmental risks while generating new opportunities to create a more sustainable global economy by installing energy efficient equipment, using lower-carbon fuels and on-site renewable energy generation, and optimizing the distribution of energy, waste and water.
