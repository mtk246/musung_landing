MUSUNG GARMENT HAS DECADES OF EXPERTISE

Musung Garment

Musung Garment has decades of expertise in readymade garment manufacturing which began with the philosophy of expertise, excellence, and elegance. As a leading forerunner of garment industry, we offer unparalleled services and receive satisfaction from clients by providing exceptional quality of products that are attentively manufactured, inspected, and distributed by our seasoned employees who have paramount specialty in garment industry.

With our state-of-the-art and high-productivity manufacturing system in our three independent factory facilities; Musung, New Green Land, and Mega One, we rely on built in mechanism to process order, manufacture products, and distribute overseas. Having employee educational training facility within our factory, we pride ourselves in forging impeccable workforce whom are responsible for serving the best due diligence for our clients. We take clients’ expectations as our top priorities and we provide foremost work environments for employees by carefully looking over their concerns.

As Musung in Korean means “limitless prolificacy”, we are striving to achieve unprecedented accomplishments and to build everlasting community that lasts over centuries.
